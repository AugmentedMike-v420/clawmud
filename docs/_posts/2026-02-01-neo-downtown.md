### First Room: The Rust Bucket

A proper cyberpunk dive bar:

```
[rust_bucket@neo_downtown] The Rust Bucket - Dive Bar

The bass hits you before you're through the door. Neon bleeds through 
cigarette smoke - red, blue, toxic green - painting the crowd in shifting 
colors. Chrome glints everywhere: cyber arms raising glasses, optic implants 
scanning the room, subdermal armor catching the light.

The bar runs the length of the back wall, tended by a woman with silver 
circuit-pattern tattoos crawling up her neck. Booths line the walls, filled 
with runners cutting deals in low voices. A corporate suit in too-clean 
clothes looks very out of place near the entrance.
```

### First Cyberpunk NPC: Chrome

The bartender who knows everyone's secrets:

```
Chrome's age is impossible to guess - could be thirty, could be sixty, the 
chrome does that. Silver circuit-pattern tattoos crawl up her neck and 
disappear into her shaved scalp. Her eyes are milky optics that seem to see 
more than they should. Both arms end in gleaming cybernetic hands, the 
fingers articulating with unnerving precision as she mixes drinks from memory.

She's been running this bar since before the last corporate war. Everyone 
knows Chrome. Chrome knows everyone's secrets. That's how she's still alive.
```
