# CM-013: Economy System

**Type:** feature  
**Priority:** high  
**Status:** Design Complete  

## Summary
Design and implement a comprehensive economy system using eurodollars (eddies). Includes legal markets, grey markets (fixers), black markets, ripperdoc pricing, and money sinks to maintain economic tension.

## Requirements

### Currency (Eurodollars)
- [ ] Primary currency: eddies (€$)
- [ ] Storage: on person (lootable), bank (hackable), stash (discoverable)
- [ ] Denominations: coins, bills, credchips, encrypted transfers

### Income Sources
- [ ] Jobs/missions (primary) — Scale with level and risk
- [ ] Looting enemies
- [ ] Selling gear (50% value, fences 30%)
- [ ] Hacking accounts (risky)
- [ ] Passive: investments, property, business

### Job Tiers
| Tier | Levels | Pay Range |
|------|--------|-----------|
| Gutter | 1-5 | 50-200 |
| Street | 5-15 | 200-1,000 |
| Professional | 15-30 | 1,000-5,000 |
| Elite | 30-50 | 5,000-25,000 |
| Legendary | 50+ | 25,000+ |

### Expenses (Money Sinks)
- [ ] Rent (weekly, by district)
- [ ] Food (daily)
- [ ] Ammunition (per use)
- [ ] Medical costs
- [ ] Transport
- [ ] Debt interest
- [ ] Cyberware maintenance

### Lifestyle Tiers
- [ ] Street (free, penalties)
- [ ] Coffin (100/week)
- [ ] Apartment (300/week)
- [ ] Condo (750/week)
- [ ] Luxury (2,000/week)
- [ ] Penthouse (5,000+/week)

### Markets

**Legal Market:**
- [ ] Fixed prices, +20-50% markup
- [ ] Safe, traced, legal goods only

**Grey Market (Fixers):**
- [ ] Reputation-based access
- [ ] Better prices, restricted items
- [ ] Variable availability

**Black Market:**
- [ ] Military grade, rare items
- [ ] High risk, scams possible
- [ ] Requires intro/reputation

### Ripperdoc Economy
- [ ] Installation costs by doc type
- [ ] Cyberware quality tiers (street → milspec)
- [ ] Complication rates
- [ ] Maintenance costs (quality-based)

### Banking
- [ ] Account types (basic → offshore)
- [ ] Loans (bank, fixer, loan shark)
- [ ] Investments (bonds, stocks, crypto)

### Faction Economy
- [ ] Reputation affects prices
- [ ] Hostile (+100%) to Exalted (-40%)

### Zone Economics
- [ ] Different zones have price modifiers
- [ ] Corp (+50%) to Underground (-30%)

- Scarcity creates tension — money should feel tight early
- Multiple valid income paths
- Debt should be motivating, not crushing
- Late game: wealth as power, not just survival
